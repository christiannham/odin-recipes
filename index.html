Odin Recipes

Here you will find a list of (3) simple recipes that I have added to my usual rotation of meals.
I strongly recommend trying them for yourself!



  • Creamy Avocado Chicken Salad (Great for lunch or dinner)

    • If you want a cool spin on the traditional chicken salad, try this one made with avocado, adding just the right amount of creamy texture to an already delicious meal!


  • Buffalo Chicken Roll-Ups (Great for lunch or dinner)

    • These buffalo chicken roll-ups are sure to be a hit at your next party, or just a chill evening with family or friends.


  • Crackered Chicken (Great for lunch or dinner)

    • This casserole-style dish is simple to make, and provides a hearty meal with plenty of delicious leftovers!
    (I enjoy my leftovers for breakfast, paired with a glass of milk.)
