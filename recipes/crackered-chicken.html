How to make Crackered Chicken

Description

This casserole dish is sure to have you coming back for more! This meal is made with very simple ingredients, and requires little effort, aside from baking time.
I love to drizzle buffalo sauce over the top once it is finished baking, and it pairs wonderfully with fresh crescent rolls. You will be glad you tried this recipe, enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. - Chicken Breast
  • 1 - Stick of Butter
  • 16 oz. - Low-Fat Sour Cream
  • 2 cans - Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 3 sleeves - Ritz Crackers (crumbled)
  • Salt, pepper

    • Optional/Additional Toppings and Pairings

  • Shredded Cheese (as much or as little as you like)
  • Buffalo Sauce
  • Poppy Seeds
  • Bread Rolls

Steps

  1. Boil chicken until no longer pink in the middle

  2. While chicken is boiling, crush up all three sleeves of crackers, sit to the side

  3. Once chicken is fully cooked, shred chicken to desired consistency, and season with salt and pepper (to taste)

  4. Mix in sour cream and cream of chicken soup, stirring until chicken is evenly coated

  5. Lightly coat your casserole dish with oil, and pour first layer into dish, smoothing out evenly

  6. Pour a layer of cracker crumbs over the chicken, and dot with butter

  7. Repeat this process until all chicken and cracker crumbs have been used
    (I usually get three layers)

  8. Bake in the oven at 350 degrees for 30 minutes, or until casserole is bubbly and light brown

  9. Pair or top with your preferred choice, dish out as necessary, and enjoy!
