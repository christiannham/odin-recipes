How to make Buffalo Chicken Roll-Ups

Description

These buffalo chicken roll-ups make an excellent addition to any party or gathering, or even just a nice snack on a relaxing night in.
This is a simple, easy meal to prepare, and can be served as a main course or paired with other finger food delights!

Ingredients

  • 4 - Flour Tortillas
  • 1 lb. - Chicken Breast (shredded)
  • 1/2 cup - Cream Cheese (softened)
  • 1/4 cup - Ranch Dressing
  • 1/2 cup - Shredded Cheese
  • 1/4 cup - Hot Sauce (whatever brand you prefer)
  • Salt, Pepper (to taste)

    • Note:

    If serving whole, you can double (or triple) the recipe in order to have plenty for everyone.
    Make a head count and plan accordingly.

Steps

  1. Boil chicken until no longer pink in the middle
    Once chicken is cooked all the way through, shred to your desired consistency, seasoning with salt and pepper

  2. In a large mixing bowl, mix all ingredients together until you have a creamy consistency

  3. Lay out the tortillas and spread mixture evenly across all of them

  4. Roll up the tortillas and chill them in the refrigerator for 30 minutes

    5. If serving whole:

  5. Pair with taco sauce or queso, and enjoy!

    6. If cutting into bite-sized snacks:

  6. Using a sharp knife, cut each roll into 1 inch pieces, and place on serving dish with side of taco sauce or queso for dipping
