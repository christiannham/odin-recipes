How To Make Creamy Avocado Chicken Salad

Description

This is a chicken salad recipe that uses mashed avocado, creating a smooth and creamy texture, while adding in valuable nutrients to an already nutritious meal.
The color of this salad comes out green, so you can also pair it with a fun 'green eggs' recipe, substituting the usual 'ham' with chicken salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. - Chicken Breast (shredded)
  • 1 Ripe Avocado (mashed)
  • 2 Tablespoons - Plain Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Tablespoon - Lime Juice (freshly-squeezed is best)
  • 1 Tablespoon - Red Onion (finely-diced)
  • 1 Tablespoon - Cilantro (chopped)
  • Garlic salt, pepper (to taste)

    • Optional additions/pairings:

  • Saltine Crackers
  • Pita Bread
  • Potato chips of your choosing
  • Whatever else your heart (read: appetite) may desire!

Steps

  1. Boil chicken breast until no longer pink in the middle.
    Once chicken is cooked all the way through, shred to your desired texture.

  2. While chicken is cooking, mash the avocado in a mixing bowl.
    Then, stir in yogurt and lime juice.

  3. Once chicken is shredded, stir together with the avocado mix you prepared in the mixing bowl.

  4. Add your diced red onion, chopped cilantro, garlic salt, and pepper.
    Stir entire mixture together thoroughly.

  5. Dish out as needed, and enjoy!!
